Payment Notes

Main Payment Method: Use card for most purchases.

Currency Tip: If a card machine asks, choose MXN instead of USD.

Cash: Keep some pesos for beach vendors, tips, parking, and water taxis.

USD: Can be accepted in some places, but usually gives a worse exchange rate.

Best plan: card most of the time + some MXN cash for smaller purchases.
