Cabo Trip Itinerary

*Times are approximate and can be updated once flights, reservations, and activity bookings are confirmed*

Saturday, March 14: Travel to Cabo

Morning / Afternoon: Flight from Los Angeles (LAX) ✈️ Los Cabos International Airport (SJD).

Arrival: Pick up rental car at the airport.

Afternoon: Stop for groceries (Walmart, Costco, or another nearby store).

Afternoon: Check in to Airbnb and get settled.

Evening: Relax at the Airbnb pool and keep the first night easy.

Dinner idea: casual tacos nearby or Tacos Gardenias.

Sunday, March 15: Medano Beach Day

Morning: Breakfast at the Airbnb.

Late Morning: Head to Medano Beach for a laid-back beach day.

Daytime: Swim, relax, walk the beach, and rent umbrella / chairs if wanted.

Afternoon: Return to Airbnb for more relaxing or pool time.

Evening: Walk around the Cabo Marina.

Dinner near the marina or downtown Cabo.

Monday, March 16: El Arco + Lover's Beach

Morning: Drive to Cabo San Lucas Marina.

Morning: Take a water taxi or boat ride to El Arco and Lover's Beach.

Explore: View Divorce Beach from a safe distance (do not swim there).

Afternoon: Lunch near the marina.

Afternoon: Return to Airbnb for pool time and rest.

Relaxed evening.

Tuesday, March 17: Day Trip to La Paz

Morning (Around 8:00 AM): Drive from Cabo to La Paz (~2 hours).

Late Morning: Visit Playa Balandra and enjoy the views.

Midday: Explore the La Paz Malecón.

Lunch: Have lunch in La Paz at a local seafood spot or café.

Afternoon: Walk around town and enjoy a relaxed waterfront afternoon.

Drive back to Cabo in the evening.

Wednesday, March 18: Camel Ride Adventure Package

Morning: Book a camel ride package tour.

Package Option 1: Camel ride + ATV desert tour.

Package Option 2: Camel ride + boat trip to El Arco.

Suggested Companies: Cactus Tours or Wild Canyon Adventures.

Afternoon: Return to Airbnb and relax after the activity.

Keep dinner casual and enjoy a slower evening.

Thursday, March 19: Online Exam + San José del Cabo Art Walk

8:00 AM – 11:30 AM: Online exam at the Airbnb.

Around 12:00 PM: Check out / leave Airbnb after exam is finished.

Afternoon: Drive to San José del Cabo.

Afternoon: Walk around the historic plaza, coffee stop, and browse the area.

5:00 PM: Enjoy the San José del Cabo Art Walk.

Dinner in San José del Cabo after the Art Walk.

Friday, March 20: Massage Day + El Farallón Dinner

Morning: Sleep in and enjoy a slow start to the day.

Late Morning / Midday: Book a private couples massage for you and your girlfriend.

Massage Ideas: Eden's Spa, Just In Time Massage, or a mobile massage at the Airbnb.

Afternoon: Relax, explore the marina, or do souvenir shopping.

Evening: Get ready for a nicer dinner.

Dinner reservation at El Farallón for sunset if possible.

Saturday, March 21: Departure

Morning: Breakfast, finish packing, and get ready to leave.

Late Morning / Afternoon: Drive to Los Cabos International Airport (SJD).

Flight: Return flight from Cabo ✈️ Los Angeles (LAX).

End of trip 🌴

Trip Notes

Trip Dates: Saturday, March 14 – Saturday, March 21.

Stay: Airbnb in Cabo area with pool.

Transportation: Rental car for airport, beaches, La Paz, and San José del Cabo.

Main Plans: Medano Beach, El Arco, La Paz day trip, camel ride package, San José Art Walk, massage day.

Special Dinner: El Farallón.

Money: Use card most of the time, but keep some MXN cash for smaller purchases.

Camel ride day can stay flexible depending on whether you book the ATV combo or boat combo.
