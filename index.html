Vancouver / Victoria Trip Itinerary

*Times are approximate and will be updated once flights / ferry are confirmed*

Thursday, November 6: Fly to Seattle

TBD (Afternoon): Depart from San Luis Obispo (SBP) ✈️ Seattle (SEA).

TBD: Arrive in Seattle.

TBD: Shuttle / rideshare to hotel near SEA, check in and rest.

Overnight: Hotel near Seattle Airport.

Notes: Pick up rental car next morning, not tonight.

Friday, November 7: Drive to Vancouver / Check-in

Morning (TBD): Pick up rental car at/near SEA.

Morning/Late Morning: Drive from Seattle, WA → West Vancouver, BC (~3 hrs driving + border).

TBD (Afternoon): Arrive at Airbnb in West Vancouver and check in.

Afternoon/Evening: Groceries / settle in / casual dinner nearby.

Border reminder: All passengers need passports ready for US→Canada and return.

Saturday, November 8: Vancouver Day

Morning (TBD): Breakfast in Vancouver.

Late Morning - Afternoon: Explore Vancouver (Stanley Park seawall, Capilano / suspension bridge area, Granville Island, etc.).

Evening: Dinner in the city and relax back at Airbnb.

Parking in downtown Vancouver can be tight and paid — plan for that.

Sunday, November 9: Day Trip to Victoria (Ferry)

Early Morning (TBD): Drive to ferry terminal.

TBD: Ferry to Victoria, BC (Vancouver Island). ⛴️

Daytime: Walk around downtown Victoria (Inner Harbour, Parliament buildings area, shops / food).

Evening (TBD): Ferry back to mainland and return to Airbnb.

Reminder: Bring passports with you for ferry/ID checks.

Monday, November 10: Free Day in Vancouver

Options: Shopping / sightseeing / chill day. Ideas: Gastown, Science World, English Bay, Lions Gate views, etc.

Evening: Pack up for checkout and drive back the next morning.

This is also a flex day in case weather moves the Victoria ferry plan.

Tuesday, November 11: Return Home

Morning (TBD): Check out of Airbnb in West Vancouver.

Morning/Late Morning: Drive from Vancouver, BC → Seattle, WA (border crossing back into U.S.).

TBD (Afternoon/Evening): Return rental car at SEA.

TBD: Flight Seattle (SEA) ✈️ San Luis Obispo (SBP).

+30 min drive: Drive from SBP back to Santa Maria (home).

End of trip 💙

Trip Notes

Hotel (11/6): Seattle airport area (TBD).

Airbnb (11/7–11/11): West Vancouver (address / check-in code TBD).

Rental Car: Pickup at SEA 11/7 morning, return 11/11 at SEA.

Documents: Passports required for border + ferry.

Currency: CAD for Vancouver / Victoria (card is usually fine but note foreign transaction fees).
